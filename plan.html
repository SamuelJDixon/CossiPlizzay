Goals

The main goals of this project are to create a website that spreads the word of the company CossiePlay,
thus increasing its costume sales and also increasing the number of people that come to the monthly cosplay parties.

Success Evaluation

Success for this website will be measured in two major ways. The first is the number of coustumes ordered per week, we expect to see a significant increase.
The second way success will be measured is the attendance of the monthly cosplay party, where we expect an increase in attendance of more than 30 guests.

Target Audience

The target audience is high school students and young adults, ages 16-25. Because this is the target audience, the major design elements of the site will be aimed at a younger audience who are more tech savvy. The website will be intuitive without being handholding as this age group is know to have a solid understanding of technology.
